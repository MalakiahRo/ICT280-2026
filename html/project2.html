Space Engineers is one of my favorite games because it allows me to build and design my own ships and vehicles in space.

I have been playing this game for years, and I enjoy creating detailed builds inspired by science fiction movies and games.

One of the things I enjoy most is designing custom ships that look practical, powerful, and unique.
