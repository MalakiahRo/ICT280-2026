Space Engineers

Space Engineers is one of my favorite games because it allows me to build and design my own ships and vehicles in space.

I have been playing this game for years, and I enjoy creating detailed builds inspired by movies like Aliens.

One of my favorite projects was building an APC and a dropship that can carry it, which made the game even more fun and challenging.

What I Enjoy Most

  • Designing custom ships and vehicles
  • Building functional systems
  • Exploring space and planets
  • Playing in creative mode
